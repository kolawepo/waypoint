The problem
Without Waypoint
  • ✗ Open a new chat — AI starts from zero
  • ✗ Re-explain your stack, what's built, what's broken
  • ✗ Switch from Claude to ChatGPT — start over again
  • ✗ Hit context limit — lose all project state
  • ✗ Come back after a break — AI has no idea what changed
With Waypoint
  • ✓ Open a new chat — banner appears automatically
  • ✓ Click Continue — full context injected instantly
  • ✓ Switch platforms — Waypoint bridges the gap
  • ✓ Context resets — one click to restore
  • ✓ Come back later — picks up where you left off
How it works
STEP 01

Work normally

Use Claude or ChatGPT like you always do. Waypoint runs silently in the background, reading your AI tabs every 30 seconds.

STEP 02

Open a new chat

Waypoint detects what you were just working on from the actual conversation content and shows a prompt at the top of the page.

STEP 03

Click Continue

Your full project context is injected into the chat input. The AI immediately understands where you left off.

Features
🔍

Auto session detection

Reads conversation content from open tabs to detect your project — no manual input needed.

🔀

Cross-platform

Works across Claude and ChatGPT in either direction. Neither AI can bridge this gap on its own.

📁

Multi-project support

Save structured state for up to 5 projects — Completed, In Progress, Blocked, Next Steps.

🔒

100% local

Everything stays in your browser. No backend, no accounts, no tracking.
